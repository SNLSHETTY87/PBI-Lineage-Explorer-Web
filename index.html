Instructions & Reference

JSON Format Reference ▼

Your JSON must contain two arrays: nodes and edges.

Nodes — each node represents a Dataflow, Dataset, or Report:

FieldTypeRequiredDescription
NodeIdTextYesUnique identifier
NodeNameTextYesDisplay name
NodeTypeTextYesDataflow, Dataset, or Report
WorkspaceTextYesWorkspace name
RefreshTimeTextNoISO 8601 datetime (e.g. 2026-02-24T06:00:00Z)
RefreshStatusTextNosuccess, failed, or progress
PbiUrlTextNoURL to open in Power BI Service

Edges — each edge connects a source node to a target node:

FieldTypeRequiredDescription
SourceIdTextYesNodeId of the upstream item
TargetIdTextYesNodeId of the downstream item

DAX Measures (for Power BI) ▼

Create these two DAX measures in your Power BI model to generate the JSON:

NodesJSON (CONCATENATEX)

NodesJSON =
VAR _rows =
    CONCATENATEX(
        Nodes,
        "{"
            & """NodeId"":"""      & SUBSTITUTE(Nodes[NodeId],"""","'") & ""","
            & """NodeName"":"""    & SUBSTITUTE(SUBSTITUTE(Nodes[NodeName],"""","'"),"&","&amp;") & ""","
            & """NodeType"":"""    & Nodes[NodeType]    & ""","
            & """Workspace"":"""   & SUBSTITUTE(Nodes[Workspace],"""","'") & ""","
            & """RefreshTime"":""" & IF(ISBLANK(Nodes[RefreshTime]),"",FORMAT(Nodes[RefreshTime],"YYYY-MM-DDTHH:mm:SS") & "Z") & ""","
            & """RefreshStatus"":""" & IF(ISBLANK(Nodes[RefreshStatus]),"",Nodes[RefreshStatus]) & ""","
            & """PbiUrl"":"""      & IF(ISBLANK(Nodes[PbiUrl]),"",Nodes[PbiUrl]) & """"
        & "}",
        ","
    )
RETURN "[" & _rows & "]"

EdgesJSON (CONCATENATEX)

EdgesJSON =
VAR _rows =
    CONCATENATEX(
        Edges,
        "{"
            & """SourceId"":""" & SUBSTITUTE([SourceId],"""","'") & ""","
            & """TargetId"":""" & SUBSTITUTE([TargetId],"""","'") & """"
        & "}",
        ","
    )
RETURN "[" & _rows & "]"

NodesJSON (TOJSON Alternative) (Power BI May 2024+, recommended for large models):

NodesJSON_TOJSON =
TOJSON(
    SELECTCOLUMNS(
        Nodes,
        "NodeId",       Nodes[NodeId],
        "NodeName",     Nodes[NodeName],
        "NodeType",     Nodes[NodeType],
        "Workspace",    Nodes[Workspace],
        "RefreshTime",  IF(ISBLANK(Nodes[RefreshTime]),BLANK(),FORMAT(Nodes[RefreshTime],"yyyy-MM-ddTHH:mm:ss") & "Z"),
        "RefreshStatus",IF(ISBLANK(Nodes[RefreshStatus]),BLANK(),Nodes[RefreshStatus]),
        "PbiUrl",       IF(ISBLANK(Nodes[PbiUrl]),BLANK(),Nodes[PbiUrl])
    ),
    100000
)

EdgesJSON (TOJSON Alternative):

EdgesJSON_TOJSON =
TOJSON(
    SELECTCOLUMNS(
        Edges,
        "SourceId", Edges[SourceId],
        "TargetId", Edges[TargetId]
    ),
    100000
)
Troubleshooting ▼
IssueFix
No edges visibleCheck that SourceId/TargetId values exactly match NodeId values (case-sensitive)
RefreshStatus bars not showingValues must be lowercase: success, failed, or progress
JSON parse errorValidate your JSON at jsonlint.com — common issues: trailing commas, unquoted keys
Nodes overlap or misalignedEnsure edges form a DAG (no circular dependencies)
